# Game Systems & Core Loop

## Core Loop
Roll → Progress → Reward (dice/rolls, cash, board completion)

## Currencies
- **Rolls** (dice): primary action currency
- **Cash**: board progression currency
- **Stickers**: collectible / album currency

## Board Progression & Feature Unlocks

| Board | Unlocks |
|-------|---------|
| Board 1 | Core Loop: Landmarks, Shutdowns, Shields, Bank Heist, Net Worth |
| Board 2 | Supporting: Community Chest, Albums, Offers, 4 Flash Events, Quick Wins, Daily Treats |
| Board 3 | Tentpole Events: Milestones, More Offers, 2 Flash Events |
| Board 4 | Social Gameplay: Tournaments, Social Minigames, Social Flash Events |
| Board 5 | Solo Minigames + High Value Flash Events |

## Key Features

### Flash Events
Board Rush, Builders Bash, Cash Boost, Cash Grab, Colour Wheel Boost, Roll Rush

### Solo Minigames
Prize Drop (Plinko), Boutique Bingo

### Albums
Sticker collection with per-season completion tracking. Album completion % strongly predicts retention (completers near 100% retention, <5% completion near 0%).

### Tournaments
Ranked competitive play. Tournament rank affects D1 retention for Occasionals only.

### Seasons
Themed seasons with IP tie-ins: Bon Appetit, Cozy Comforts, Harry Potter, Pets.
IP-driven seasons (e.g., Harry Potter) are powerful re-entry triggers for lapsed players.

## FTUE & New User Funnel

Download → Auth (89%) → Narrative onboarding (73%) → Landmarks (68%) → Shutdown (66%) → Shields (59%) → Bank Heist (56%)

- 33% total drop during FTUE, 11% at authentication step
- End of D0: 67% still on Board 1, 86% within Boards 1-2
- New users don't understand purpose of cash → cash offers harmful for new users
- 20-29% of sessions end due to Out of Rolls (OOR) or Out of Cash (OOC)
- iOS users show better retention (25 extra dice for Apple login)

## Reactivation Profile
- 24% of reactivated users are Board Level 1 (never progressed past early game)
- 50% are BL9+
- Season starts show higher desire to stay
- IP-driven seasons are powerful re-entry triggers
