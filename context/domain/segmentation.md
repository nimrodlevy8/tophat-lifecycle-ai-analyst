# Segmentation Framework

## Lifecycle Levers

### Tenure Buckets
- D0-D6, D7-D14, D15-D30, D31-D90, D91-D180, D181-D365, D365+
- 71% of DAU are D365+ tenure

### Inactivity Length (Rich Returns)
- Early: 6-13 days inactive
- Middle: 14-20 days inactive
- Late: 21+ days inactive
- Best reactivation outcomes within 28 days of churn

### Acquisition Channel
- Paid vs Organic vs Social (Friend invite)

## Activity Segments
- **Always Regular**: plays 7 days/week
- **Almost Regular**: near-daily but misses some days
- **Casual**: moderate frequency
- **Occasional**: ~450k WAU, 71% weekly return rate, 70% are former regulars
- **Funnel**: in onboarding / early progression
- **Reactivation**: recently returned from inactivity
- **Returning Reactivation**: returned and sustained

Key splits:
- 70% of players play 7 days/week ("Regular"), 30% non-regular
- WAU composition: 75.9% Returning, 9.9% New, 14.2% Reactivated

## Behavioral Levers (Engagement Engine)

1. **Win Rate**: 0% minigame win rate audience (4M WAU, $0.26 ARPDAU); below-avg tournament rank; album completion at 100%
2. **Depth of Engagement**: <95% album completion; disengaged from event with >=24hrs remaining
3. **Activity**: Occasionals, Player Profiler, Churn Prediction model
4. **Social**: Social Score, Active Social Network (ASN) — social score inversely correlates with churn (low score ~5-6% churn, high score <1%)
5. **Monetization**: Disengaged from Loyal Payer program
