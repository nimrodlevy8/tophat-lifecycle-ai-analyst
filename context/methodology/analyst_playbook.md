## Segmenting Reactivations

<!-- NEEDS INPUT: How should the analyst think about reactivated users?
Some prompts:

- How do you define reactivation vs. just a returning player? (Minimum days absent?)
- What's the right way to bucket reactivated users? (By inactivity length? By where they left off? By what brought them back?)
- What metrics matter most for reactivation analysis? (D1 survival? D7 survival? Re-churn rate?)
- How do you compare reactivation cohorts fairly? (Season-start reactivations vs. mid-season?)
- What's the difference between a "good" reactivation and a "bad" one? (Someone who stays vs. bounces?)
- Rich Returns segments (Early/Middle/Late) — when do you use these vs. custom cuts?
- Do you treat reactivated payers differently from reactivated non-payers?
-->
