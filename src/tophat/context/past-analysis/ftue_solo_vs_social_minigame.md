# FTUE Solo vs Social Minigame Analysis

**Date:** August 2025
**Analyst:** Kelly Raas

## Purpose
Evaluate whether introducing a fixed solo minigame during FTUE improves early retention vs defaulting to LiveOps calendar.

## Data
- 3M players reaching board 4-5 between April-June 2025 who triggered a LiveOps event
- Dx Retention measured from minigame enter date

## Why Solo First?
- 67% of players have zero friends at Board 4
- Solo minigames provide clear goals without dependency on others
- Ensure mechanics comprehension before social complexity
- Build confidence and intrinsic motivation

## Recommendation
Implement a curated solo minigame as part of FTUE. Ensure experience is intuitive, rewarding, and progression-linked.
