# Metric Definitions

## New Users

All metrics anchored on Day 0 = first activity date.

### Retention
- **D1 Retention** — users active on day 1 / users active on day 0
- **D7 Retention** — users active on day 7 / users active on day 0
- **D14 Retention** — users active on day 14 / users active on day 0
- **D30 Retention** — users active on day 30 / users active on day 0

### ARPI (Average Revenue Per Install)
- **D1 ARPI** — sum of revenue through day 1 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D7 ARPI** — sum of revenue through day 7 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D14 ARPI** — sum of revenue through day 14 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D30 ARPI** — sum of revenue through day 30 (inclusive) / users active on day 0

### Conversion
- **D1 Conversion** — users who made a payment through day 1 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D7 Conversion** — users who made a payment through day 7 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D14 Conversion** — users who made a payment through day 14 (inclusive) / users active on day 0
- **D30 Conversion** — users who made a payment through day 30 (inclusive) / users active on day 0

## Reactivations

Same structure as new users, but:
- **Day 0** = reactivation date (not first activity date)
- Dx metrics measured from reactivation date forward

## General Gaming KPIs

Standard mobile gaming KPIs (ARPDAU, ARPDAP, DAU, WAU, payer rate, etc.) follow industry-standard definitions. See `standard_main_kpis` table for computed values.
