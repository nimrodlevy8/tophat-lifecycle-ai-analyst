# Lifecycle Mission & Vision

## Mission
Strive for audience stabilization and growth by delivering more personalized, data-informed experiences that maximize player value across every stage of the journey — from onboarding to re-engagement — while fostering early and long-term retention.

## Product Principles

1. **Elevate Onboarding** — Design rewarding, intuitive onboarding for new and reactivated users. Accelerate engagement to drive early and reactivation retention.
2. **Understand Lifecycle Segments** — Develop deeper insight into inflow/outflow audiences (qual + quant). Collaborate with EE to shape product strategy across verticals.
3. **Clarify Value Early** — Collaborate with Marketing & UA to surface relevant benefits through personalized, timely, frictionless experiences.
4. **Drive Continued Engagement** — Create systems that sustain momentum beyond early sessions using smart triggers, personalized content, and timely nudges.

## Execution Pillars

1. **RTUE Foundation** — Permanent development track for incrementally improving RTUE/FTUE experiences
2. **Lifecycle-Based CRM** — Tailored communications and offers at each stage to enhance retention
3. **Waves of Reactivation** — Orient reactivation efforts to content release tentpoles; ensure consistent in/out-of-app experience

## Guiding KPIs
- DAU / WAU
- Retention (Early & React)
- Regularity / Habituation
- Reactivation Volume

---

## 2025 Strategic Learnings

- ~½ of players: top churn drivers revolve around **player-perceived difficulty**
- ~⅓ of players returned because **friends/family asked them to trade stickers or be co-op partners**
- ~⅓ of players returned out of **curiosity for what's new**

**Fundamentals:**
- RTUE audiences are more diverse than other audiences
- LiveOps calendars apply to all audiences — hard to adjust RTUE/FTUE experiences independently
- Difficult to address volume vs quality — limited ability to stem audience decline

- D30 Retention decreased by **64% since game launch**

- **Biggest contributor: D1**, with consistent decreases D3-D30
- Android data biased by Jul-Oct 2025 suspicious traffic surge

## Reactivation Segments

| Segment | Definition | Volume (% inactive) | React Rate | D7 React Retention | Opportunity |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Recent Churn | Inactive 7-14 days | 5.5M (2.6%) | 1.2% | 30.3% | Low (already performs well) |
| High Value Churners | 30+ active days or past regulars/customers, inactive >14d | 36M (17.5%) | 0.19% | 20.5% | **High** |
| FTUE Churner | Churned before Board 5 | 129M (61.9%) | 0.03% | 9.2% | **High** (huge audience, ignored) |
| Socially Engaged Churners | ≥10 friends at churn | 5.5M (2.7%) | 0.11% | 13.6% | Medium |

### Segment Approaches
- **Recent Churn:** CRM automation, extend RTUE Quick Wins
- **High Value Churners:** RTUE Solo Minigame, Special Offer, Quick Wins Catch Up, "While You Were Away"
- **FTUE Churner:** RTUE at return, unlock RTUE solo + RR at BL3, CRM re-onboarding, FTUE Quick Wins, unlock strategy testing
- **Socially Engaged:** RTUE Social Reactivation, social CRM/UA messaging, optimize Friendly Nudge
