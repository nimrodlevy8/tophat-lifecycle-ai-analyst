# Deep Dive

## When to use
Structured multi-section investigation into a topic.

## Methodology
1. Frame the question and define scope.
2. Establish baseline: what does "normal" look like for this metric/behavior?
3. Identify dimensions to cut by (time, segment, feature, geo).
4. For each dimension:
   - Pull data
   - Identify patterns or outliers
   - Form hypotheses
5. Test top hypotheses with targeted queries.
6. Synthesize findings into a narrative:
   - What happened
   - Why (root cause or contributing factors)
   - So what (impact quantification)
   - Now what (recommendations)
